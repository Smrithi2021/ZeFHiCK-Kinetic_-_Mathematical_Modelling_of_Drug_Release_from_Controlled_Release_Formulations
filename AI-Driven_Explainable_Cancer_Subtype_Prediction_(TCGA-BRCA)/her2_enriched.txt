HER2-enriched breast cancer is characterized by amplification or overexpression
of the ERBB2 (HER2) gene. These tumors often lack estrogen and progesterone
receptor expression and show high proliferation rates.

Key genes associated with HER2-enriched subtype include ERBB2, GRB7, FGFR4,
and CDK12. This subtype responds well to HER2-targeted therapies such as
trastuzumab and lapatinib.

TCGA studies have shown that HER2-enriched tumors cluster separately
from Luminal subtypes at the transcriptomic level.
